OUR RECIPE

2 servings

30 minutes

INGREDIENTS

    • 2 cups uncooked long-grain rice
    • 2 cups tomato puree or crushed tomatoes
    • 1 large onion, chopped
    • 2 - 3 Scotch bonnet peppers or habanero peppers, chopped (optional)
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
    • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
    • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
    • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
    • 2 cups beef or chicken broth
    • 2 bay leaves (optional)
    • 2 sprigs fresh thyme (optional)

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Sauté onions, tomatoes, and Scotch bonnet peppers (or substitute with habanero) until the onions are translucent.
  3. Add grated ginger and garlic and sauté for 1 minute.
  4. Add tomato paste and stir well, cooking for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add rice, stir to coat with tomato mixture, then add water or broth and bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20-25 minutes or until rice is cooked and liquid is absorbed.
  7. Season with salt, curry powder, thyme, and cumin to taste.
  8. Stir in chopped bell peppers and continue cooking for an additional 5 minutes.
  9. Serve hot, garnished with chopped fresh parsley or cilantro, and enjoy with protein of choice (e.g., chicken, beef, or fish).

Jollof Rice + Chicken

2 servings

30 minutes

Nigerian Jollof is a beloved West African dish consisting of flavorful one-pot rice, typically cooked with tomatoes, onions, peppers, and spices. Its origins are a subject of friendly rivalry among African nations.

It's popularly believed to have originated in Ghana, but Nigeria's unique variation has gained widespread acclaim and loyalty across the continent and beyond.

NOTES

Handle rice gently to prevent clumping. When stirring, avoid pressing down on the rice mixture. Use a thermometer to ensure the liquid has reached a proper boiling temperature (212°F).

For optimal results, maintain a steady simmer and monitor the moisture level.

Note: These instructions are tailored specifically for Jollof Rice preparation..
